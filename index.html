Wanhua Li

I am a fourth year Ph.D student in the Department of Automation at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Jiwen Lu and Prof. Jianjiang Feng .

In 2017, I received my B.S. degree in computer science at Sun Yat-sen University, Guangzhou, China.

My research interests lie in computer vision and deep learning, particularly social relation recognition, age estimation and graph neural networks.
